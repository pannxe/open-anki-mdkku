Guildline for Making Anki Card

Understand, then memorize

Cloze Deletion & Image Occusion Only - Easy to make, easy to recall

Simple is Better - Simple Deck : do not based deck on lecture, but on related topic. Simple card, one fact per card, concise wording

Images = God - Provide image in the card if it is logical to do so

Ordered List - provide direction or order if the answer has multiple item. Forcing us to recite the order the same way each time

This guildline is largely based on Med School Insiders' video
